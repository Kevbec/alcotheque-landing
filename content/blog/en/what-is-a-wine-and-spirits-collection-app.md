## What Is a Wine and Spirits Collection App?

A wine and spirits collection app is a mobile tool that helps you manage every bottle you own — from the first scan to the last pour.

Instead of relying on memory or a handwritten list, the app keeps a complete, searchable record of your collection at all times.

## What You Can Do With It

- **Scan bottle labels** using your phone camera and AI to automatically fill in the name, type, and vintage
- **Track quantities** so you always know what you have in stock
- **Organize by location** — wine fridge, cellar, home bar, or any custom storage spot
- **Record gift bottles** with the name of the person who gave them
- **View analytics** like your most collected spirit types or your collection growth over time

## Who Is It For?

A collection app is useful for anyone who owns more than a few bottles and wants to stay organized. This includes casual wine lovers, home bar enthusiasts, whiskey collectors, and serious cellar managers.

## Wine vs Spirits: Does the App Cover Both?

Most apps focus on wine only. Alcotheque is built for both wine and spirits in a single inventory — which matters if your collection is mixed.

## How AI Scanning Works

You point your camera at a bottle label. The app reads the label using optical character recognition (OCR) and pre-fills the bottle details automatically. You review and save. The whole process takes a few seconds.

## Is It Free?

Most collection apps offer a free tier with a limited number of bottles, and a paid subscription for unlimited access. Alcotheque allows up to 15 bottles for free with no time limit.
